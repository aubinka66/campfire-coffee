Fresh Beans or GTFO

Fresh coffee beans are the first and most important ingredient when it comes to brewing great tasting coffee. When you look around your local supermarket it's easy to assume that the coffee you see on the shelves is fresh. This is usually far from being the case. Coffee beans are roasted, allowed to cool, ground and then put in cans or packages. By the time you take them home, it may be weeks or even months since the beans were roasted. That's a problem, because coffee beans begin to lose their flavor the moment they come out of the roaster.
